About AirTaxi

Welcome to AirTaxi, the future of urban air mobility. Founded in 2020, we're revolutionizing transportation with our fleet of state-of-the-art electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft.

Our mission is to provide fast, safe, and sustainable air transportation that reduces urban congestion and travel time. With zero emissions and whisper-quiet operation, we're setting new standards for eco-friendly travel.

All our pilots undergo rigorous training and our aircraft are maintained to the highest safety standards. We're proud to have completed over 10,000 flights with a 100% safety record.

Join us in shaping the future of transportation and experience the freedom of urban air mobility.
